The new CAD model reduced complexity and unecessary features, better preserved design intent, and introduced different configurations to the model. I worked to improve the reliability of device operation, making modifications that improved its accuracy and robustness. In addition to the mechanical design, I assisted George in fabricating the disposable silicone chips used to load reagents into the device.

I am now beginning DFM analysis and packaging of an external imaging module.
